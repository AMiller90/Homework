What is a High Warlord?

A High Warlord Is A Rank In The PVP Ranking System Of World Of Warcraft. To Achieve This Status You Would
Farm Kills In The Battlegrounds.It Usually Takes At The Very Least 10 Hours a day of PVP non-stop for about 
6 months.There Can Be Only One High Warlord Per Server.Once The Status is attained it is attained forever.
The Achievement Can Be Obtained by having a rating of 2400 as part of the horde.
